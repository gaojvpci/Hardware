Page by: Jack Gao, Daniel Liacos, Tory Chen and Zaki Rangwala.

Our Page is about our Hardware Project assigned to us in our Introduction to Computer Science class, and what we're doing is taking apart a computer and reassembling it, to learn about its parts, where they go, and how they function.
Our first Page consists of pictures of us taking the computer apart, and examining the components and what they might be, and what role they play in the Computer.
Our second page is about putting the PC together, and the right parts in the right places after we've learned what each component does.
Our third page is about our project, and what our goal. Just an overview of the project.
Our last page is about the parts of the PC, and what part each of them plays in the whole PC.
