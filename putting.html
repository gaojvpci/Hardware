Putting the Computer Back Together

First, we put the easy-to-install components back on, like the Floppy Disk Drive and the I/O Board. Next, we put the hing screws in to connect both parts again.

Photo of Floppy Disk Drive after we put it in

Photo of Hinge screws being put in

Next, we put the Mother Board back on.

Finally, we put the Mother Board back on the panel.

Finally, we got the cover back on the Power Supply Unit after lots of hard work (bending and hammering the cover so it fit), and slid it back into place on the panel.

We wedged the fan in under the cover of the power supply.

Finally, we put the covers back on the PC, and we finished with a (99%) whole PC!

All done! Just need to close the cover.
